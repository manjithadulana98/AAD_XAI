AAD XAI Starter — Results Log
============================

How to use this file
- Each run/experiment gets a new dated entry appended at the end.
- Record: method/protocol, key settings, and mean accuracy (± std) + what you’re comparing against.
- If you compare across protocols (different CV, lags, overlap), note “not directly comparable”.
